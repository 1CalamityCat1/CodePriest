Our Story

At CodePriest, we believe that the future of technology lies at the intersection of the physical and digital worlds. Founded by Thapelo Fata, our mission is to bridge the gap between complex hardware and scalable software by providing specialized engineering solutions for Target Market, Industrial Manufacturers and Tech Startups. While many firms focus solely on web applications, we thrive in the realm of Specific Technical Niche,Embedded Systems and IoT Integration, ensuring that every line of code we write serves a tangible, real-world purpose. Our journey began in Zimbabwe Harare with a vision to "exorcise" the technical inefficiencies and security vulnerabilities that hold businesses back. By combining Primary Programming Language, C++/Python expertise with advanced Specific Hardware, PCB Design or ESP32 Development, we empower our clients to automate their workflows and secure their infrastructure. Whether we are building a custom MVP for a growing startup or deploying a robust sensor network for a factory, CodePriest remains committed to Core Value,Engineering Excellence and Technical Integrity.
